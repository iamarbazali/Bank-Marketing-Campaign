# Bank-Marketing-Campaign
This project analyzes a bank telemarketing campaign using an interactive Tableau dashboard. It focuses on how customer and campaign attributes relate to subscription conversions.
Key features

KPI cards for total contacts, overall conversion rate and average call duration.
​

Calls by day of week to see when campaigns engage the most customers.
​

Conversion by job role to highlight high and low performing customer segments.
​

Education versus subscription view to compare contact volume and conversion across education levels.
​

Month by marital status heatmap to spot seasonal and demographic conversion patterns.
​

What I did

Cleaned and prepared the telemarketing dataset and defined calculated fields for conversion rate and duration based metrics.
​

Built interactive filters for job, education, month and day of week to support targeted campaign analysis.
​

Designed the layout and visual choices to help marketers compare segments and identify where to focus future campaigns.
